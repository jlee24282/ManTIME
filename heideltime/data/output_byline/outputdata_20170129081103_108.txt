Highland’s Konnor Cordingley (138), Snake River’s Ceaton Howell (170) and Payson Anderton (195) and Blackfoot’s Jake Stewart (220) all took second. Pocatello’s top placers were Hunter Roubidoux (145) in third with Jorsh Ronquillo (98) and Tanner Tinsley (160) in fourth.

JOHN BERRY CLASSIC 
   
Shelley crowned three champions en route to a third-place finish at Saturday’s John Berry Classic tournament at Sugar-Salem High School. Darrick Stacey (113 pounds), Caleb Call (132) and Braxton Balmforth (145) each won their respective weight classifications. 
   
Malad took seventh, led by top-four finishes from Riggin Smith (98), Chet Potter (132), Hazen Smith (152), Rhett Daniels (220) and Austin Atkinson (285). 
   
Other area second-place finishers include Bear Lake’s Daniel Holmquist (106), Shelley’s Christian Lamb (120), Grace’s Bracken Anderson (132) and Grace’s Mark McGregor (195). 
   
Sugar-Salem won the tournament with 207.5 points. 
   
NORTH IDAHO RUMBLE 
   
American Falls placed four wrestlers in the top six of Saturday’s North Idaho Rumble at Coeur d’Alene High School. Boone Giulio (138 pounds) took third, Russell Adkins (132) finished fourth, and teammates Cameron Henesh (170) and John Blair (220) were sixth. 
   
The Beavers finished 11th. Post Falls won the tournament with 258.5 points.
