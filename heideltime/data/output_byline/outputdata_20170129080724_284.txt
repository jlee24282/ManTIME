Cavanaugh earns RBC Wealth Management honor 
 
PORTSMOUTH — Timothy Cavanaugh, a senior vice president, financial advisor in RBC Wealth Management’s Portsmouth office, was again <TIMEX3 tid="t1" type="DATE" value="PAST_REF">recently</TIMEX3> selected as a member of the firm’s Senior Consulting Group, an exclusive group of advisors recognized for their success in building consultative, fee-based businesses. The group is one of the highest levels of recognition that the firm bestows upon its fee-based financial advisors. 
 
To qualify, Cavanaugh demonstrated a commitment to a consultant-based investment practice, helped clients build investment portfolios and maintained an excellent record of customer service. 
 
RBC Wealth Management financial advisors assist individual, corporate and institutional clients by providing a wide array of wealth management solutions such as stocks, taxable and tax-exempt bonds, options and mutual funds. It also assists clients with retirement plans and money management programs. 
 
In the United States, RBC Wealth Management operates as a division of RBC Capital Markets, LLC. Founded in <TIMEX3 tid="t2" type="DATE" value="1909">1909</TIMEX3>, RBC Capital Markets, LLC is a member of the New York Stock Exchange, the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority, Securities Investor Protection Corporation and other major securities exchanges. RBC Wealth Management has $273 billion in total client assets with 1,900 financial advisors in 200 locations in 41 states.
