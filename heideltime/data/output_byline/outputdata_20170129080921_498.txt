In continuing drive against smuggling of foreign liquor into 'dry' Bihar, rail police seized huge consignment of foreign and country liquor and arrested five liquor peddlers from a bogie of an express train at railway station here late <TIMEX3 tid="t1" type="TIME" value="2017-01-28TNI">last night</TIMEX3>. Rail police said here that 75 bottles of foreign liquor and 700 pouches of country liquor were seized from five bootleggers inside a bogie of Maldah-Anand Vihar Express following a tip off. The consignment was being smuggled from Sahebganj in Jharkhand when it was intercepted by rail police. An intensive interrogation of liquor peddlers is on to nab other members of their gang.
