Gospel Music Extravaganza    
The Lytle Scholarship Benefit Concert presents Gospel Music Extravaganza, featuring the UC San Diego Gospel Choir as led by Ken Anderson. Proceeds from ticket sales and 100% of donations benefit the Lytle Scholarship Endowment, which provides scholarship support for graduates of The Preuss School at UC San Diego attending Thurgood Marshall College at UC San Diego.
