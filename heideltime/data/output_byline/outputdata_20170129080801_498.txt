FAIRBANKS — Northrim Bank announced the promotion of eight women in its retail banking department, in Alaska, according to a news release.  
   
Three women were promoted to vice president positions within the bank: Cheryl Fellman was promoted to regional sales and service operations manager, Tammy Kosa to regional sales and development manager and Darci Ornellas as the VP regional sales and consumer lending manager 
   
Five women were promoted to associate vice president positions: Anita DeVore was promoted to regional sales and service manager, Erin Gage to retail banking operations manager, Jill Milham to retail banking operations assistant manager, Gerlie Monta-Guevarra to branch manager II at the Ketchikan Financial Center and Diana Soliday to branch manager at Wasilla Financial Center.
