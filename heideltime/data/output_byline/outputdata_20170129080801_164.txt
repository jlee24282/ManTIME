Wodonga stayed in touch on the back of Ashley Bates defeating Greg Robbins 27-21 and Peter McLarty’s 27-21 victory over Laurie McDonald. 

In a round of upsets, Wangaratta edged out second-placed Corowa Civic 103-102.
