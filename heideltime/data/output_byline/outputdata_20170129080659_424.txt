Wararka ka imaanaya Deegaanka Kulbiyow dhanka Kenya ayaa ku waramaya in mid kamid ah diyaaradaha helicopterka Ciidamada Malatariga Kenya lagu soo  riday deegaankaas. 
   
Saraakiil ka tirsan Maamulka Jubba-land  ayaa xaqiijiyay in diyaarada ay sameynaysay Howlo roondeen ah iyo in deegaankaas ay ka difaacdo 
 
dagaalyahanada Xarakada Al-shabaab oo shalay weerar ku qaaday Saldhiga Ciidamada Kenya ee deegaanka Kulbiyow. 
 
Saraakiisha Maamulka Jubba-land  ayaa hadalkooda intaas ku daray in diyaarada qoriga baasuukaha loo yaqaano ay ku dhifteen Ciidamada Al-shabaab oo wali ku harsan deegaanka Kulbiyow ee Gobolka Jubadda Hoose, waxaa haatan la xaqiijiyay in diyaarada ay soo dhacday.
