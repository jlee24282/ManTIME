ALEXANDRIA, La. (KALB) - The ASH Trojan powerlifting team hosted their annual, ASH Invitational <TIMEX3 tid="t1" type="DATE" value="2017-01-28">Saturday</TIMEX3>. Lifters began competition at <TIMEX3 tid="t2" type="TIME" value="2017-01-28T09:00">9:00 a.m.</TIMEX3> and the meet lasted until <TIMEX3 tid="t3" type="TIME" value="2017-01-28T18:00">6:00 p.m.</TIMEX3> 
   KALB   
The Trojans were competing against powerlifting teams from Tioga, Buckeye, Peabody and Natchitoches Central. ASH asserted their dominance in the sport once again, as both the boys and the girls powerlifting teams were victorious. The winning didn't stop there for the school as their powerlifters claimed three of four individual honors. 
 
ASH swept both of the outstanding lifter girls’ platforms. Zyanna Price earned it as a lightweight lifter and Lily Poisso got it as a heavyweight lifter. The boys were split. The outstanding lifter in the boys light platform was ASH's Ken Brown. University Academy's James Lofton was the outstanding lifter for the boys’ heavy platform.
