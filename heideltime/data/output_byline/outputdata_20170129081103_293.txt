US President Donald Trump and German Chancellor Angela Merkel had a telephone conversation. Political leaders discussed the activity of NATO, the situation in the Middle East, and the conflict in the East of Ukraine. 
 
This was announced by the spokesman for the German government, Steffen Seibert on Twitter page, radio "Svoboda" reported.

"A wide range of issues was considered, including NATO, the situation in the Middle East and North Africa, relations with Russia and the conflict in eastern Ukraine.
