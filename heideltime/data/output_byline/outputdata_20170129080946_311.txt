A DROITWICH voluntary organisation is holding an official launch of a new minibus available to the community. 
 
The Community Transport Scheme at Droitwich Spa CVS, an independent local voluntary organisation that supports voluntary action, received a new minibus from the Department of Transport.

Tonia Enderbury, Head of Services at Droitwich Spa CVS, said: “If you would like to find out more about Droitwich Community Transport and the services it runs locally please come along and have a chat and a look at the new minibus. 
 
“We are very keen to speak to local charitable organisations who would like to use our minibus, or anyone that would be interested in becoming a volunteer for this local community scheme.”
