ISLAMABAD: The National Transmission and Despatch Company (NTDC) has connected 1,320MW Sahiwal Coal-fired power plant with the national grid through a 500kV transmission line under the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC) framework. 
 
According to a statement, the construction work of the 5km long transmission line was completed in <TIMEX3 tid="t3" type="DATE" value="2016-11">November 2016</TIMEX3> ahead of its deadline.

The power will be evacuated through 500kV grid station Yousafwala and 500kV grid station Lahore.
