The Jack in the Box Triple Bacon Buttery Jack Burger is Indulgent  
  By: Michael Hemsworth -  <TIMEX3 tid="t3" type="DATE" value="2017-01-29">Jan 29, 2017</TIMEX3> 
References: brandeating  Set to be available at participating locations starting on <TIMEX3 tid="t5" type="DATE" value="2017-02-02">February 2</TIMEX3>, the Jack in the Box Triple Bacon Buttery Jack Burger is a new indulgent offering from the QSR brand. 
The new burger features bacon butter, bacon mayo, hickory-smoked bacon strips, a signature beef patty, grilled onions and cheddar cheese on the brand's signature bun. 

The burger comes as the brand's first endeavor of utilizing bacon butter in its offerings to offer bacon lovers a triple hit of bacon in every bite of the indulgent sandwich. 

While the Jack in the Box Triple Bacon Buttery Jack Burger is set to be available at <TIMEX3 tid="t8" type="DATE" value="2016-02" mod="START">the beginning of February</TIMEX3> the sandwich is purported to be available for only a limited-time.
