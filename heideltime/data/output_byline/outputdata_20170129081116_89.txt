The Executive Director of the Economic and Organised Crimes Office Mr. Justice Tsar Yao has been dismissed by President Akufo-Addo, Starrfmonline.com has gathered.

Information gathered by Starrfmonline.com indicates that the President is yet to appoint a substantive head of the agency specialized in monitoring and investigating economic and organised crime.

In <TIMEX3 tid="t3" type="DATE" value="2016-06">June last year</TIMEX3>, Justice Tsar, who was one of the Deputy Executive Directors, was appointed Acting Executive Director to replace Biadela Kweku Mortey Akpadzi, whose contract had elapsed after his retirement.
