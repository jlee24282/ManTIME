Wildwood promotion 
 
Bevan Keating, who has been the artistic director of the Wildwood Academy of Music & the Arts, has been named as artistic director for its parent organization, Wildwood Park for the Arts in west Little Rock. 
 
Keating will be in charge of the park's expanding music and arts education programs and development of its concerts and events in the 625-seat Lucy Lockett Cabe Festival Theatre, the Art in the Park visual arts exhibitions and a series of seasonal festivals. 
 
Keating, a native of London, Ontario, has been collaborating with Wildwood on several stage productions, some of them with Praeclara, a multidisciplinary performing arts company based at Little Rock's Second Presbyterian Church, where Keating is also the director of music. 
 
He is also director of the choral and voice department at University of Arkansas at Little Rock, and conducts the UALR Concert Choirs, the UALR Chamber Chorus and Women's Chorus and the UALR Community Chorus. 
 
Keating succeeds Cliff Fannin Baker, who also served as Wildwood chief executive officer, and Wildwood founding artistic director, Ann Chotard. Leslie Golden is the park's executive director.
