The selected acts will go on to perform in front of a live audience on <TIMEX3 tid="t10" type="DATE" value="2017-03-01">Wednesday, March 1</TIMEX3>, and <TIMEX3 tid="t11" type="DATE" value="2017-03-02">Thursday, March 2</TIMEX3>, at Central Arizona College, 8470 N. Overfield Road.

For more information call Raylene Johns at 421-8677, ext. 4523.
