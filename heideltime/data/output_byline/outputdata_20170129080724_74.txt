Guy Adrian Sands passed away in the early morning hours of <TIMEX3 tid="t6" type="DATE" value="2017-01-24">Tuesday, January 24, 2017</TIMEX3>. Guy was born in Republic, Washington, and was a long time resident of the Glide area. Guy was a Korean War veteran, and at times a logger, expert faller, a miner and eventually retired from the U.S. Forest Service in Glide. 
 
He is survived by two sons, Jason Sands of Roseburg, and Martin Sands of Idleyld Park; two grandchildren; and two great-grandchildren. Also surviving Guy is his two sisters, Donna Favor of Idaho, and Phyllis Bean of Washington. 
 
At Guy's request no service is planned.
