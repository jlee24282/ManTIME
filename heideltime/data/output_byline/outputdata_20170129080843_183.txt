Kuwait's Health Minister Dr. Jamal Al-Harbi affirmed <TIMEX3 tid="t5" type="DATE" value="2017-01-28">Saturday</TIMEX3> the ministry's keenness on supporting the Kuwait Health Office in Germany, to overcome all obstacles . 
Al-Harbi made his remarks to KUNA while visiting the Kuwait Health Office in downtown Frankfurt, where he was received by the Office's director Dr. Mubarak Al-Qabandi and staff . 
The minister also pointed out the discussions he has held with Al-Qabandi regarding the office's efforts, indicating that he has also inspected the office workflow and listened to a comprehensive explanation on its <TIMEX3 tid="t6" type="DATE" value="PAST_REF">recent</TIMEX3> activities and development plans . 
He also expressed his admiration toward the office's website and e-system, which contribute to facilitating all procedures for Kuwaiti patients in German hospitals, reiterating the ministry's keenness on meeting the office needs . 
Meanwhile, the Kuwaiti minister, accompanied by Al-Qabandi and the health office's staff, visited Kuwaiti patients in Median Kaiserberg Clinic, located in southcentral Frankfurt, during which they checked on the patients' condition and the hospital's facilities, in addition to conducting consultations with the medical team in charge of treating the Kuwaiti patients . 
Al-Harbi also talked with the Kuwaiti patients and listened to their requests, pledging to meet all their needs, cooperate, and communicate with them closely . 
He also expressed his gratitude to the director and staff of the Kuwait Health Office in Frankfurt, valuing their efforts in providing all forms of assistances for the Kuwaiti patients in German hospitals.
