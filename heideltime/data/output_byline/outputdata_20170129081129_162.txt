About Time of Grace  
Time of Grace is for people who want more growth and less struggle in their spiritual walk. Through the timeless truth of God's Word, we connect people to God's grace so they know they are loved and forgiven and so they can start living in the freedom they've always wanted. Time of Grace shares the news of God's amazing grace through television, print, and digital media.
