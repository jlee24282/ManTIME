Floyd Mayweather has expressed his belief that a superfight with Conor McGregor WILL happen. 
 
The retired boxer did not confirm where or when, but gave the clearest indication yet that the fight may take place. 
 
Speak at the MGM Grand in Las Vegas ahead of Carl Frampton's rematch with Leo Santa Cruz, Mayweather said: "Can we make this fight happen? Absolutely. It's what everyone wants to see. 
 
"The fans want this fight. The fans have been asking for this fight. It's all about entertainment. He's very entertaining. He's very outspoken like myself, so let's give the fans what they want to see.
