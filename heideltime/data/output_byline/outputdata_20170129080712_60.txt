Punjab Chief Minister Shahbaz Sharif has said steps are being taken expeditiously for the uplift of healthcare system. 
 
He stated this while talking to a high level delegation of Health Ministry of Turkey, led by its Director General Dr Oner Guner, in Lahore. 
 
They also discussed matters related to progress on the Reforms Program for improvement in the Healthcare System of the Province. 
 
The Chief Minister said access to quality medical facilities in hospitals is a right of the common man. 
 
Dr. Oner Guner said that all-out cooperation will be extended to Punjab for improvement of healthcare system.
