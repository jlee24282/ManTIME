Massachusetts State Police are on-scene of a fatal crash on Route 495 northbound in Norton. 
 
Preliminary investigation by troopers from the Foxborough Barracks indicates that a <TIMEX3 tid="t1" type="DATE" value="2004">2004</TIMEX3> GMC Sierra pickup truck being operated northbound by a 52-year-old Franklin man went out of control and rolled over north of Exit 10. 
 
The driver, the lone occupant of the truck, sadly, was determined to be deceased at the scene. His name is not being released at this time. 
 
The entire northbound side of the highway remains closed at this time as troopers process the scene. Northbound traffic is being diverted at Exit 10. 
 
The cause of the crash remains under investigation by Troop H of the Massachusetts State Police with assistance of the State Police Collision Analysis and Reconstruction Section, State Police Crime Scene Services Section, and State Police detectives. 
 
Troopers were assisted on scene by the Norton Police and Fire Departments and the Massachusetts Department of Transportation. 
 
No further information is being released at this time.
