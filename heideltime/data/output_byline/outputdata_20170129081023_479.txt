MALTA – The Pilots and Trojans played a back-and-forth game, but once Raft River elected to switch from a 2-3 zone defense to full-court man-to-man, Glenns Ferry turned the ball over several times and allowed the Trojan to find some breathing room. Raft River attempted 31 free throws on the night, and converted on 17. Sophomore Audah Jones led Raft River with 18 points. Senior Jentry Solosabal led Glenns Ferry with 11 points, and junior Jenni Rodriguez added 10. Junior Sage Wootan also had a monster rebounding night for Glenns Ferry, finishing with 25 boards, according to head coach Rick Hance.

The win secures the fourth seed in the Snake River Conference Tournament for Raft River, and the loss locks Glenns Ferry into the sixth seed.
