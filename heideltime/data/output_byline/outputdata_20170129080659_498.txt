A 48-year-old professional classical dancer collapsed and died while performing Bharathanatyam with his guru on stage, during a temple festival in Paravur near here. Omanakuttan was rushed to a private hospital in Paravur but was declared brought dead. Local TV channels aired the video purportedly showing the dancer collapsing during the stage performance.
