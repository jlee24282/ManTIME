A former union council member in Lakshmipur has been shot dead by robbers at his residence. A group of robbers stormed the house of Shofiullah Sabu, 55, at Andar Manik village during the wee hours on <TIMEX3 tid="t1" type="DATE" value="2017-01-29">Sunday</TIMEX3>.

As Shofiullah challenged them, the robbers stabbed him with sharp weapons and then shot him dead. They fled away with gold jewellery and other valuables, said Shofiullah's son Alauddin.

Shafiullah was a former member of Tewariganj Union ward 7.

Hearing him scream, neighbours reached the house and found his blood-drenched body, said Tewariganj Union Council Chairman Omar Faruq Bhulu.

Police recovered the body <TIMEX3 tid="t2" type="TIME" value="2017-01-30TMO">next morning</TIMEX3> and sent it to the morgue of state-run Lakshmipur Sadar Hospital.
