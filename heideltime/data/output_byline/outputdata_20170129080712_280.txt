Occupants continue to attack Ukrainian positions in all directions, including the use of heavy weaponry. This was reported by ATO HQ on Facebook . 
 
In Mariupol direction, Shyrokyne, Talakivka, Lebedynske and Vodyane were shelled from 122 mm artillery. Mortars of various calibers were used to attack Krasnohorivka, Shyrokyne and Novotroitske. Occupants used rocket launchers and small arms to attack Shyrokyne, Talakivka, Marinka, Novotroitske, Lebedynske and Vodyane. Enemy snipers worked in Novotroitske and Hnutove. Enemy APCs also repeatedly attacked Shyrokyne. 
 
In Donetsk direction, militants fired 120 and 82 mm mortars, rocket launchers of various systems and small arms at Avdiivka, Kamyanka, Zaytseve, Luhanske, Troitske. 
 
In Luhansk direction, Russia-backed occupation forces shelled Krymske with 122 mm artillery, fired rocket launchers at Troitske and Novozvanivka.
