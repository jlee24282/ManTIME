Manama :  Bahrain National Triathlon Team won the first leg of the Triple Crown Ironman Championship held in Dubai as the Bahraini team qualified for the World Championship to be held in Barcelona. 
 
“The awards the Bahraini teams received at the closing ceremony of the Triple Crown Ironman Championship reflects the advanced level our Bahraini youth have reached thanks to the unwavering and support from HM the King,” said HH Shaikh Nasser bin Hamad Al Khalifa, Representative of His Majesty the King for Charity Works and Youth Affairs, Chairman of the Supreme Council for Youth and Sports, President of Bahrain Olympic Committee.

“The Championship attracted a large number of young athletes from Bahrain, who occupied top places. This asserts the popularity of triathlon sport in the region, and it gives us motivation to continue our work to extend its outreach,” Shaikh Nasser highlighted.  
 
HH Shaikh Khalid bin Hamad Al Khalifa, First Deputy President of the Supreme Council for Youth and Sports, President of West Asian Athletics Federation, President of Bahrain Athletics Association, congratulated Shaikh Nasser for his achievement in the Championship.
