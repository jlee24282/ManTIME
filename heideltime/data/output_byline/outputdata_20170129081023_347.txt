Bangkok: Four people have been killed and five wounded after a grenade exploded during a fight between two rival gangs in a province in eastern Bangkok.

According to police, a fight broke out between the two teen gangs early on <TIMEX3 tid="t1" type="DATE" value="2017-01-29">Sunday</TIMEX3> during the celebration of a festival in eastern Bangkok's Chachoengsao province.

One of the boys threw a grenade at members of the rival gang, which left four people dead and wounded another five, four of whom have been admitted to a hospital in the area with serious injuries, The Nation daily reported.

Police have not reported any arrests in connection with the incident so far.
