Hyderabad: Mr. Mohammed Abdul Razzaq Superintendent District R&B office Vinarpati was presented award on the occasion of 68th Republic Day. District Collector Mrs. Shweta Mohanty and district SP Mrs. Rohini Priyadarshni gave away the award. Present on the occasion were Vice Chairman State Planning Commission Niranjan Reddy, MLA G Chinna Reddy and Joint Collector Niranjan.
