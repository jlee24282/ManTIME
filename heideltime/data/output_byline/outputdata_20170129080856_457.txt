Finance Minister Ishaq Dar has directed the National Savings to continue providing better services to the general public and improve culture of savings in the country. 
 
He said this while chairing a meeting on National Savings in Islamabad on <TIMEX3 tid="t1" type="DATE" value="2017-01-29">Sunday</TIMEX3>. 
 
Director General National Savings Zafar Masood briefed the finance minister on initiatives undertaken by National Savings. The initiatives include improvement in customer services, development of new products, human resource management, organization's brand activation, and strategic improvements in the organization. 
 
He apprised the minister that new products like registered prize bonds or being planned for roll-out, based on the feedback and demand of investors.
