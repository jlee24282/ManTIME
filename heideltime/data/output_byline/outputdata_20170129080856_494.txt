HAVERHILL — The Northern Essex men's basketball team rallied from a 10-point halftime deficit to defeat Springfield Tech, 76-70. It was only the Knights' third win of the season. 
 
Haverhill's Isaiah Ruffen and Eddir Cardonal led the Knights with 20 and 15 points respectively while Adam Goldstein of North Reading and Grant Bellino had 11 points each and Lawrence's Felix Moore added 10. 
 
It was only the first win of the second semester for the Knights, who are down to eight players on their roster.
