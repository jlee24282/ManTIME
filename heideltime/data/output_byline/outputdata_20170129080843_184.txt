Director of the Albanian National Tourism Agency Ardit Collaku has praised Kuwait ' s contributions to his country through Kuwait Fund for Arab Economic Development (KFAED), saying " We aspire for more bilateral cooperation and Kuwaiti investment in Albania ." Speaking to KUNA on the sidelines of a visit by a group of talented Kuwaiti female students, Collaku said that Albania sought to attract Kuwaiti tourists and was eager to become an appealing tourist destination, pointing out that Albania enjoyed many advantages such as its geographical location in central Albania and picturesque nature and most importantly being an Islamic country . 
On his part, Charge d ' Affairs of Kuwait ' s Embassy here and second secretary Saud Al-Mutairi said that KFAED ' s projects contribute to the strengthening of relations with Albania and serve the foreign policy of the State of Kuwait .
