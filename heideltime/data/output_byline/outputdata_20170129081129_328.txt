More than 60 vendors are scheduled to attend the fourth annual Camarillo Ranch wedding showcase from noon to <TIMEX3 tid="t12" type="TIME" value="2017-01-28T16:00">4 p.m.</TIMEX3> <TIMEX3 tid="t11" type="DATE" value="2017-02-11">Feb. 11</TIMEX3>, organizers said. 
 
Partnering with Camarillo Ranch are Party Pleasers Event Rentals, Camarillo Bridal Boutique and Unique Floral Designs. Organizers said the aim is to create a luxurious, unique and memorable experience for local brides and grooms-to-be, as well as their family and friends. 
 
The event at 201 Camarillo Ranch Road in Camarillo will include a bridal fashion show and grooms’ dance-off. Other  highlights will include: 
  Wedding industry vendors, including rentals, caterers, florists, photographers, disc jockeys and more.  Wedding bingo and raffle prizes valued at $100 each.  Food, dessert and alcohol samples from caterers, bakeries and wineries.  Wedding design inspiration in all Camarillo Ranch event spaces.  A grand prize of a complimentary wedding and reception hosted at the Camarillo Ranch, valued up to $6,500 (restrictions apply).
