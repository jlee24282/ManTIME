The Chipotle 'Cado Crusher' Game Rewards with Free Chips and Guac  
 The Chipotle 'Cado Crusher' game is a new promotional experience created by the QSR brand that rewards players with free chips and guac when they play. The new promotional game was created in partnership with Avocados from Mexico and focuses on highlighting the very few simple ingredients that are present within the brand's dip. 
The Chipotle 'Cado Crusher' game will provide players with a mobile offer of free chips and guac after they have played through the game. The coupon comes through to users via text and can be redeemed when heading into a participating Chipotle location and purchasing an entree.
