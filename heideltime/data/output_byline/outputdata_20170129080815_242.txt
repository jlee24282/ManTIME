Women in 18th-century Malta were under the control of husbands, brothers, fathers or even sons. Yet a substantial number of these women managed to overcome the restrictions imposed on them by law and society. Ms Mifsud Bonnici is a senior lecturer in Systems of Knowledge at the Junior College.
