Assembly elections in Uttar Pradesh are around the corner and this would be the first outing of the two leaders after the alliance between the Congress and the Samajwadi Party was announced. As per sources, the roadshow will begin from the GPO Park and will go through the Mayfair roundabout to Lalbagh, Novelty roundabout and then weave its way to Qaiserbagh through Muslim dominated areas like Aminabad and old city. Later Gandhi and Yadav will go to Nakkhas, Chowk, Ghantaghar where the road show will conclude. Police have made security arrangements made for the high-profile road show.
