She was recognized as the SSSD Employee of the Year and a Scranton Times Northeast Woman in 1986. She donated faithfully to multiple charities throughout her life. After retirement, she was a devoted volunteer at Marian Convent. Janet most loved spending time with her family, including her many trips with them across the country, especially to the shore at Stone Harbor.
