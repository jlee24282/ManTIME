The fund was established in the aftermath of the murders of Michael’s daughter, Lena, and two grandchildren, Mason and Christian, and now sets out to help children and families in need. &#10; &#10;“We wanted them to be remembered in a positive light,” Michael said, adding that the fund had its first fundraiser in 2006 and that members include family members, volunteers and members of the AFL-CIO Union Council.
