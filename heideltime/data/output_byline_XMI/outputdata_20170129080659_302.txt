Because like Hope Place, it had its ducks in a row.&#10;&#10;Last week, the local leaders responsible for Hope Place, a new facility in Daytona Beach for homeless families with children, held a groundbreaking ceremony at the former Hurst Elementary School. It was symbolically important, not just for what it means for Hope Place, but also for showing the community what can  and must  be accomplished with regard to other sectors of homelessness.&#10;&#10;The dozens of community volunteers, social service administrators and elected officials crowded into a small, former classroom represented the collective support that was required to make Hope Place a reality.
