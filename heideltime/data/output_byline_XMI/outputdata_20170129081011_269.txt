Iaria says this condition may affect up to 2 percent of the population. &#10; &#10;DTD was brought to light by filmmaker Michelle Coomber in the 2010 documentary &quot;Lost Every Day,&quot; about Sharon Roseman of Denver, who was unable to find her way around her own apartment. &#10; &#10;Roseman had kept her condition a secret for years after receiving several wrong diagnoses, including multiple personality disorder.
