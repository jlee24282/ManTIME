There he was, silently sitting. He was courageous and never afraid of natural disasters. When he saw the devastation around, he wanted to see the areas around. So, he and I went around the City, Prabesh Man recalled. &#10; &#10;He was also a wrestler. He used to have bouts with many people at the same time. He regularly did push-ups and exercised too.
